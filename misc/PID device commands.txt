0 - Setpoint
1 - N Kp
2 - N Ki
3 - N Kd
4 - Amplifier (TK)
5 - Integral (Threshold)
6 - Current averaging (<>0)
7 - Input channel (0..2: ADC, SPI, DS)
8 - Control info out (7 lines, starting with ">D:")
9 - Log (<>0)
T - Default ambient (edsTemp)
A - Mode (0..3: N,A,D,M)
P - A Kp
I - A Ki
D - A Kd
S - Sensor indexes (first two numbers after sign)
C - Current calibration
V - Device info out (3 lines, starting with ">I:")
B - Distill bias
L - Current DS indexes (2 lines, starting with ">S:")
W - Manual mode power
O - Tolerated overshoot for d-mode

J - CJC enable
N - Enable cooler output
